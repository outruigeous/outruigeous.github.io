About me

I'm Mingz — a UX Designer and Researcher.

I've spent the last 5 years working in cross-functional teams across banking and government-tech, untangling complex problems and designing solutions that actually work for the people using them.

My biggest career joys come from collaboration — the kind where you get into a room with engineers, policy folks, and end users and figure out something that none of you could have reached alone. I care deeply about the people I design for and the people I design with.

I'm looking for end-to-end product design, UX research and strategy, and systems work — especially in complex or enterprise spaces where the problems are genuinely hard.

What I bring

End-to-end product design, UX research (qualitative and quantitative), service design, design ops, and workshop facilitation. I'm comfortable moving between strategy and execution, and between ambiguous early-stage problems and mature systems that need refinement.
